About the School

Meridian Madhapur campus first opened its door in 2006. Following the CBSE curriculum the school has grown leaps and bounds year to year. We now have strength of 1200+ children from grades Nursery to Class X. We have also been awarded reaccreditation from the British Council, and we are the British Council School ambassadors.

At Meridian we are committed to developing students with a well rounded personality who are true global citizens with international understanding, academic excellence, sensitive to the environment and to fellow beings and who are equipped to be leaders in their chosen field.
